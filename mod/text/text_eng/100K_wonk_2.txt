This is the plans for a series of buildings made to manufacture a multitude of products.

They were large towers with a multitude of large chimney stacks extending out of them. 

A wide variety of products were meant to be manufactured there, ranging from simple chemicals to complex machines. 

8 buildings were planned to be built but only one was finished.

This was due to the multiple accidents occurring while the buildings were being maintained or built, due to the height of the buildings. <LINE>Causing the project to be halted.
